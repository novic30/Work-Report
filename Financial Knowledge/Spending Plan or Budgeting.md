- An outline of expenses by category for a specified period of time (weekly, monthly, annually)
- It considers income from Job, Gifts, Allowance, investments and loans.
- And it considers expenses which is about where the money goes


Fixed Expenses: The ones which dont vary very often.
- Cell Phone Bill
- Fixed Transportation Bill
- Bus Pass
- Subscriptions

Variable Expenses: Things that go up and down
- Groceries
- Utility Bill

Cash Flow: Intersection b/w income and expenses (Summary of income & Expenses).
- Based on specific time period, like weekly, monthly.
- Positive cash flow is more money coming in than expenses.
- Negative is more expenses than income
